About us

Free Code Camp Helsinki is interested in helping people pay more taxes. Specifically, we want to help you get an awesome, well-paying job as a developer. We meet together every couple weeks to learn the skills you need to start a new career in software development. We are not officially affiliated with freecodecamp.com, but we love the work that they do and often send our members their way. They have fantastic resources over there, so check them out if you haven't already!
